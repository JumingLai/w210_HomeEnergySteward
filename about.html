About

Visualization goal:

Analyze and Visualize Electricity Demand by time, by living space and by appliance
Learn effect of weather on electricity usage

Intended audience:

Energy stakeholders including utility companies and homeowners

Data source:

Sourced from Kaggle SMART HOME DATA WITH WEATHER INFORMATION.
The dataset has elaborate time-series data for electricity usage in a home for 2016.
It has readings for multiple devices and multiple areas of a home. Weather data for the years 2016. Sampled by the hour.
Data quality: Both overall power usage and temperature readings are clean, without nulls or outliers.
